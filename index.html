Game of Thrones

Since its debut in 2011, HBO's Game of Thrones has become one of the most popular and critically acclaimed shows on TV. With an intricate storyline and an amazing cast, how could you not be hooked? The show is based on the book series A Song of Ice and Fire by George R. R. Martin. The show takes place in the fictional lands of Westeros and Essos and follows the lives of the leading families in Westeros competing for the throne (hence the "game of thrones").
